# MyVoiceBook

Offline speech-to-text notes and a system voice keyboard for Android. All recognition runs on your device — no cloud.

- [Privacy policy](privacy/)
- [Feature wishlist](wishlist.md)

**MyVoiceBook** is available on Google Play. Free tier includes hold-to-talk dictation with small on-device models; Pro unlocks hands-free sessions, longer capture, and additional models.
