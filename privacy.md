# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 9, 2026

MyVoiceBook ("the app") is developed by Paul Craciunoiu. This policy describes how the app handles information on your device.

## Summary

MyVoiceBook is designed for privacy: speech recognition runs **entirely on your device**. We do not operate servers that receive your audio or dictated text.

## Information the app uses

### Microphone

The app uses the microphone to capture speech for on-device transcription in the notes editor and in the system voice keyboard (IME). Audio is processed locally and is not uploaded to us or third-party speech services.

### Text you dictate or type

Notes are stored locally on your device in an app database. When you use the voice keyboard in other apps, the IME may read surrounding text in the active field **only on your device** to choose spacing and capitalization. That text is not transmitted off your device.

### Internet access

Internet access is used for:

- **Optional speech model downloads** that you initiate in Settings (files are verified with SHA-256 before use)
- **Refreshing the in-app feature wishlist** from this site
- **Google Play Billing** when you purchase Pro (handled by Google)

Internet is **not** required for dictation once a model is installed.

### Purchases

If you buy MyVoiceBook Pro, Google Play processes the transaction. We receive purchase confirmation through Google Play Billing to unlock Pro features on your device. We do not receive your payment card details.

## What we do not collect

- No analytics or advertising SDKs
- No cloud speech recognition
- No upload of audio, transcripts, or keystrokes to our servers
- No account sign-in required for core features

## Data retention

Notes and downloaded models remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app. Android backup may include app data if enabled on your device; we do not control device backup behavior.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do.
